## About This Project

This repository is my Digital Literacy Portfolio, submitted as part of the
CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The project required me to take on
the role of a Student Digital Ambassador — someone responsible for helping
peers navigate the digital world safely, professionally, and responsibly.

The portfolio is organized across five tasks, each mapped to a module of the
course, covering everything from digital awareness and professional online
presence to cybercrime prevention and email etiquette.

## Task Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic covering five core digital literacy topics:
what digital literacy is, useful digital tools for students, safe internet
practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. Designed to
be informative and easy to understand for first-year students.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on GitHub and LinkedIn. GitHub profile README
introduces my background, what I am currently exploring, and my goals as
an AI student. LinkedIn Education section is updated with my current
programme and institution.

- GitHub: [github.com/sathaytejareddy](https://github.com/sathaytejareddy)
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/sathya-teja-reddy-73698337a/

### Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms
Created an account on HackerRank and completed a beginner-level coding
challenge. Also built a five-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using
Google Forms, linked to a Google Sheet for response tracking.

- Google Form Quiz: [Add your form link here]

### Task 4 – Professional Email and Etiquette Guide
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting an assignment deadline
extension from a professor, and one expressing interest in a summer
internship opportunity. Also created a social media do's and don'ts
checklist for responsible online behaviour as a college student.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study and Prevention Guide
Wrote a detailed case study on phishing, covering what it is, how it
happens step by step, who gets targeted, and the consequences. Also
created a ten-point prevention checklist with specific tips on UPI and
financial safety, and reporting channels including cybercrime.gov.in
and helpline 1930.
